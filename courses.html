Courses and Fees

Puri Bahasa Indonesia offers a wide range of courses designed to suit students of all levels, from communicative beginner (A1) to advanced (C2), and to meet particular professional requirements or interests.

See All Courses

See Courses' Fees

Classes are organised either on a one-to-one or on a larger group basis, and fees are structured accordingly. Below is level courses and fees, but we stress that we are flexible as to the hours a student may wish to take General Indonesian Conversation and Indonesian for special purposes.

Levels

These levels: Communicative beginner / A1, Post Elementary / A2, Post Elementary / A2, Intermediate / B2, Pre Advanced / C1, Advanced / C2 are available. Each of them are to be completed for the length of time of 50 hours.

Indonesian For Special Purposes

This course is suited to the students particular needs and purposes. It lasts for 50 hours.

Short Course

This intensive course lasts for 36 hours, possibly taken 6 or 3 hours, or only 6 days, and is designed to equip the student with a functional command of the Indonesian language.
